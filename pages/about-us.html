about us

Mkup Cosmetics was born in Toronto, Canada. Makeup artist and photographer Frank Toskan and Frank Angelo, a beauty salon owner; They were frustrated by the lack of makeup that would allow them to achieve good photographs, so they decided to create their own. At first, they made the cosmetics in their kitchen and sold them directly in the beauty salon to other makeup artists, as well as to models and photographers.
